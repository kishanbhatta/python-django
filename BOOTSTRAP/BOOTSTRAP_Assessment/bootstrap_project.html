Welcome to our cafe: Coffee Lovers

You will find the best coffee in town. Authentic Koshi Coffee at your town.

Just follow the instructions steps here to order your coffee and book a tentative time to sip your coffee at cafe. Now you don't have to wait come and grab your coffee.

  1. Go to our website and download the Application.
  2. Enter your desired menu.
  3. Submit your menu & do not forget to take your KOT number.
  4. Be at our cafe at the time you have selected and grab your ready coffee.
